PRAYER MEETINGS
Ephesians 6:18
"And pray in the Spirit on all occasions with all kinds of prayers and requests. With this in mind, be alert and always keep on praying for all the Lord’s people."
Each week there are two meetings:
- Tuesday evenings (7:00 - 8:00 PM)
- Thursday mornings (9:30 - 10:30 AM)
About
During each of these meetings, we gather together as a corporate body to listen from God's word and then share group and individual prayer requests. We spend the second half of the prayer meeting in small groups in order to give as many as possible the chance to pray audibly.
